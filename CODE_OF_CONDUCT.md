# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness.
- Respecting differing opinions and experiences.
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful language.
- Personal attacks and trolling.
- Public or private intimidation.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior and may take appropriate and fair corrective action.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behavior through GitHub issues or direct maintainer contact.
